Concept for a new Trigram view (v0.7.0)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
DESCRIPTION
In dependency with the date the single lines from the Trigram, Hexagram
will generated.

Examples (Lines are from left to right == top to bottom):
 - yang = odd  numbers = +.
 - yin  = even numbers = -.
 - 28.07.1967 -> -,+,+ -> yin,yang,yang
 - 17.09.2018 -> +,+,- -> yang,yang,yin
 - 20:08:35 17.09.2018 -> -,-,+,+,+,- -> yin,yin,yang,yang,yang,yin
